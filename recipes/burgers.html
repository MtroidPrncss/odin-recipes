Description

This is a simple and delicious burger recipe that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, and cheese for toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then form into 4 patties.
  3. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a skillet for about 1-2 minutes.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the buns and adding your favorite toppings.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
